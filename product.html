Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products

What is the expected lifespan of your solar panels?

Our MSE SolarMax Pro panels are designed to last 30+ years with proper maintenance. They come with a 25-year performance warranty guaranteeing at least 80% of rated output after 25 years of use.

How much maintenance do the wind turbines require?

The MSE WindStream turbines require minimal maintenance. We recommend an annual inspection by a qualified technician. The sealed bearings are designed to last 10+ years before needing replacement.

Can the battery systems be expanded?

Yes, our MSE PowerVault systems are modular and can be expanded in 5kWh increments up to 30kWh total capacity. Additional battery modules can be added at any time.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we have a network of certified installers across the country. Installation can be arranged through our sales team and is typically completed within 2-4 weeks of order confirmation.

What financing options are available?

We offer several financing options including 0% interest plans for 12 months, 5-year loans with competitive rates, and lease-to-own programs. Government incentives and rebates may also be available in your area.
